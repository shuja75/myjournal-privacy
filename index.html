Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

MyJournal ("the App") is a self-reflection journaling app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

MyJournal is designed with privacy as a core principle. The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

The only data generated through your use of the App consists of:

  • Journal entries and conversations: The text you write and the AI-generated reflection responses you receive.
  • Knowledge entities and patterns: Insights and recurring themes derived from your journal entries to support personalized reflection.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All journal data — including conversations, knowledge entities, and patterns — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers that store your journal content. If you delete the App, your locally stored data will be removed from your device.

3. Data Shared with Third Parties

MyJournal uses the Claude API, provided by Anthropic, to generate AI-powered reflection responses. When you interact with the journaling feature, the content of your conversation is sent to Anthropic's API servers for processing.

Important details about this data sharing:

  • Conversation text is transmitted to Anthropic solely for the purpose of generating a reflection response.
  • The API key used for these requests is embedded within the App and managed by the Developer. You are not required to provide your own API key.
  • Anthropic's use of data received through its API is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy and their API Data Usage Policy. As of the effective date of this policy, Anthropic states that it does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models.
  • Beyond API calls to Anthropic, we do not share your data with any other third parties.

4. Analytics and Tracking

MyJournal does not collect analytics, usage statistics, or tracking data of any kind. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

5. Data Security

Because your journal data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from the security protections built into iOS, including device encryption and passcode/biometric protection. Data transmitted to the Anthropic API is sent over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

MyJournal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can address the concern.

7. Your Choices and Control

  • Deleting your data: Since all journal data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by deleting the App from your device.
  • Controlling API communication: The App requires an internet connection to generate AI reflection responses. If you prefer not to send data to the Anthropic API, you may choose not to use the journaling feature or use the App in airplane mode (note that AI-powered responses will be unavailable offline).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how your data is handled.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, please reach out through our App Store support page.
